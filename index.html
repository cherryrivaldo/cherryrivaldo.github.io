  • About Us

    Based in Paris, ZouGlobal Consulting is a consulting firm specialized in developing and implementing internationalisation strategies of Asian and European companies. We offer all personalized services to our clients including consultancy service, national product certification, corporate registration, selection of distribution channels, and creation of JVs.

    Our Mission

    We serve as a bridge between Asia and Europe to promote the well-being of citizens in healthcare and food.

     

    We are principally engaged in the following areas: Biotechnology, Pharmaceutical, Hospital & Clinic, Digital Health, Medical Devices, and Food and Nutrition.

    Our Story

    We have strong network resources in Asia and Europe with direct access to the research centers, pharmaceutical and food manufacturing companies, start-ups, and institutional investors.

     

    All of our consultants have lived, studied, and worked both in East Asia and in major cities of Western Europe. We aim to leverage our advantage of having concrete knowledge in two different worlds, Asia and Europe, to provide our clients with a smooth channel for business development between the two continents.

  • Our Advantages

    We are a professional team of consultants and senior advisors in West Europe (France, the UK, Belgium, Germany, and Italy) and in Asia (China and South Korea).

    We have close connections with institutions such as China Medicinal Biotech Association, Tianfu Life Science Park (78 laboratories, focusing on biotech engineering and medical data management), as well as with the government bodies such as the China Chamber of Commerce in France, Europe-China Investment Association, and the Korean Chamber of Commerce in Italy.

  • Our Projects

    We provide our clients with tailor-made consulting solutions. We provide hands-on consulting service and local implementation projects, with a long-term strategic and operational development.

     

    Our clients value the way we listen and respond to what they need, the commercial pragmatism of our people, and our total commitment to seeing them succeed.

    International Strategy, Market Access Strategy, and New Business Development

    We work closely with the regulatory bodies in Asia and Europe to tailor a product-specific strategy for regulatory approvals. We set a timeline and budgets to minimize risks and increase the probabilities of regulatory approval

    Market Evaluation, Pricing Strategy, and Sales & Channel Management

    We constantly develop international distribution channels, define and evaluate product branding strategy. Our wide network of physicians, government officials, regulatory professionals and industry experts enable us to better determine the strategy

    Mergers & Acquisition Sourcing, and Merger Integration

    We evaluate potential targets, understand value chain and estimate potential synergies and arrange face-to-face discussion between the buyer and the seller

    Financing Solution

    We connect R&D projects, start-ups, growing companies with institutional investors for pipeline development and internationalization strategy

    Partnership Projects

    We form partnership projects in technology cooperation as well as organize academic exchanges as part of the internationalisation plan. Our extensive resources benefit bilateral cooperation
